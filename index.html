About

Dual‑qualified across agriculture and technology

Gafar is a dual‑qualified professional — Plant Breeding and Computer Science — with more than 8 years working at the intersection of digital agriculture, monitoring & evaluation, and data analytics. He has deployed tools like FarmerChat and AKILIMO, onboarded 120,000+ farmers, and trained 200+ extension agents across Nigeria and Kenya.

His work spans Good Agricultural Practices (GAP), Farmers Business School (FBS), Cooperative Business School (CBS), Contract Farming arrangements and Market‑Oriented Extension Training (MOET) — backed by fluency in Python, Power BI, R, Tableau and Excel, and a UN Women gender‑mainstreaming certification.

He has delivered for donor‑funded programmes under GIZ, IITA and SAPZ, building M&E frameworks that tracked 80,000+ beneficiaries and informed continued donor investment.
